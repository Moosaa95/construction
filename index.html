Services

Engineering Construction

SRUM AND ASSOCIATE LIMITED can provide a wide range of construction services, including building construction, road construction, bridge construction, and more. The company has a team of experienced engineers and construction workers who are committed to delivering high-quality projects.

Project Supervision

SRUM AND ASSOCIATE LIMITED can supervise all stages of a construction project, from the planning phase to the final construction phase. The company's project supervisors are trained to ensure that projects are completed on time, within budget, and to the required standards.

Consultancy

SRUM AND ASSOCIATE LIMITED can provide consultancy services to clients who are planning construction projects. The company's consultants can help clients to develop project plans, identify potential risks, and create budgets.

Road Network and Drainage

SRUM AND ASSOCIATE LIMITED can provide road network construction services, including the construction of highways, streets, and alleys. Additionally, the company can provide drainage system construction services, ensuring that roads and buildings are protected from water damage.

Cost Management and Control

SRUM AND ASSOCIATE LIMITED can help clients to manage and control the costs associated with construction projects. The company's cost management experts can provide accurate cost estimates, monitor project expenses, and identify cost-saving opportunities.

Counter Project Execution

SRUM AND ASSOCIATE LIMITED can partner with other construction companies to execute counterpart projects. By collaborating with reputable construction companies known for core professionalism, the company can ensure that projects are completed to the highest standards.

Our Objectives

SRUM AND ASSOCIATE LIMITED is committed to providing high-quality construction services to clients in Nigeria. The company's objectives are to:

Security

To ensure the safety and security of our clients' properties and the people who use them, by implementing strict security protocols, conducting regular risk assessments, and providing appropriate training to our staff.

Quality

To consistently deliver high-quality construction services to our clients by using the latest engineering techniques, implementing strict quality control measures, and continuously improving our processes.

Customer Satisfaction

To exceed our clients' expectations by providing excellent customer service, responding promptly to their inquiries, and ensuring that their needs are met throughout the project lifecycle.

Sustainability

To promote sustainable practices in our operations, by minimizing our environmental impact, promoting social responsibility, and ensuring that our construction projects are designed and executed with sustainability in mind.
